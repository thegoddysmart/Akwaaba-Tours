Amazing Places to Tour and have wonderful experiences.

  • Kakum National Park

    Would you believe this national park was established by the initiative of the locals and not by the state of deparment responsible for wildlife preservation? Well you better believe it! As part of one of only 3 locations in Africa with a canopy walk-way over a thick forest- connecting seven tree tops and providing access to the thick forest, Kakum National Park is the place to be. Located in the central region of Ghana close to the coast so more site seeing on your way there!!

    Explore

  • Larabanga Mosque

    Do you love architecture or take pictures of building because they look beautiful? Tired of the south of Ghana, want to enjoy some heat and explore the northern part of Ghana? Then Larabanga close to Damongo is the place to be. This town holds the oldest mosque - LARABANGA MOSQUE in the country and one in West Africa,with a Sudanese architectural design and referred to as the Mecca of Africa, you really do need to see this beautiful masterpiece.

    Explore

  • Aburi Botanical Gardens

    You love plants, you love great scenery, you enjoy cool weather and love nature, then this is the place to be. About 45 mins drive from Kotoka International airport and not too far from Greater Accra, you head towards the mountains to land in this beautiful botanical garden - home to over 1000 plant species. Come learn about how this garden played an important role in encouraging cocoa production in the south of Ghana and also enjoy the beautiful landscape and weather.
